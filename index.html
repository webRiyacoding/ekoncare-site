Health Concerns

Cancer

Cancer, also known as malignancy, is the development of abnormal cells that divide in an uncontrolled manner and have the ability to destroy the normal body tissue.

Migraine

Migraine is a headache which is a result of specific changes within the brain. Migraine can cause severe head pain that is often accompanied by sensitivity by light, noise or any kind of smell.

Low blood pressure

Hypotension, also commonly known as low blood pressure is a condition in which blood pressure in the arteries gets abnormally low. In some cases, the blood pressure gets so low that it fails to reach all the body organs.

Urinary Tract Infection

A urinary tract infection (UTI) is an infection in any part of your urinary system- kidneys, ureters, bladders and urethra. Most infections attack the lower urinary tract, which is the bladder and urethra.

Dyslexia

Dyslexia is a neurological learning disability that affects one’s ability to identify speech sounds and how they relate to words but it has no effect on their intelligence and vision.

Diverticulitis

A digestive condition known as diverticulitis, and more especially colonic diverticulitis, is defined by the inflammation of aberrant pouches called diverticula that can form in the wall of the large intestine.
